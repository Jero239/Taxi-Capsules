About us

Innovation in Urban Mobility

Taxi Capsules is a pioneering initiative in autonomous and sustainable urban transport. We work in close partnership with Airbus, one of the world’s leading aerospace companies, to develop innovative mobility solutions.

Our vision is to revolutionize city transportation with next-generation autonomous vehicles, combining Airbus’ advanced engineering expertise with a modern and eco-friendly approach.

Together, we are building the future of urban mobility.
